Lobster

Lobsters can teoretically live forever because their cells do not get damaged when they are duplicated.

  • scientific name: Nephropidae
  • avarage lifespan: in practice they usually become 30 to 50 years old
  • habitat: Oceans

Lobsters have long bodies with muscular tails. It is a highly prized seafood that turns red after cooking. When there is shortage of food lobsters are prone to cannibalism.
